Better Living in London

Predicting the future of London's boroughs

The Factors

House Prices

House prices are a big talking point in London and we hope to predict the future prices of London's boroughs.

CO2 Levels

CO2 is a large contributor to climate change so we will be looking to predict each borough's emissions for future years.

Crime Rates

People want to live somewhere safe so we're looking to predict which boroughs will have the lowest crime rates.

Open Spaces

Sometimes it's nice to have a break from the concrete jungle so we're looking at which boroughs have the most open spaces.
